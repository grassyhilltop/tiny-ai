1 · The problem, in numbers

Five real observations from the (toy) clinic. Effectiveness peaks near the middle: the Goldilocks zone. No straight line can fit a bump, and that single fact is why neural networks need a nonlinearity. Two ReLU "hinges" can bend a line into a bump.
